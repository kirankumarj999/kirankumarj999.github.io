## üöÄ Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Deploy Your Landing Page

**Upload these 3 files to hosting:**
- link-in-bio.html

**Free Hosting Options:**
- **GitHub Pages** (easiest): https://pages.github.com
- **Netlify** (best for custom domain): https://netlify.com
- **Vercel** (fastest): https://vercel.com

**Detailed instructions in README.md**

---

### Step 2: Get Your Link

Once deployed, you'll get a URL like:
- `https://your-username.github.io/project/link-in-bio.html`
- `https://your-site.netlify.app`
- `https://your-site.vercel.app`

**Shorten it** using:
- Bit.ly: https://bitly.com
- TinyURL: https://tinyurl.com
